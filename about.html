About Red Ifat Camel Canada

Your trusted partner in premium legume exports, connecting Canadian quality with East African markets through sustainable and reliable distribution.

Our Mission

To provide East African wholesalers, retailers, restaurants, and government programs with access to the highest quality Canadian red lentils, chickpeas, kidney beans, and specialty legumes. We are committed to sustainable sourcing, reliable international supply chains, and building lasting partnerships that nourish communities.

Our Vision

To become East Africa's leading importer of premium Canadian legumes, recognized for our unwavering commitment to quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction. We envision a future where nutritious, responsibly sourced legumes from Canada are accessible throughout East African markets.
